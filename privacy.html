Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 23, 2026

EuPosto is a personal video publishing tool. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information.

Data We Collect

EuPosto does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All data (API tokens, video files) remains on your local device.

API Tokens

Your API tokens are stored exclusively in your browser's localStorage and are only used to communicate directly with the respective platform APIs (YouTube, Instagram, Facebook, TikTok).

Third-Party Services

EuPosto connects to the following third-party APIs on your behalf: YouTube Data API v3, Instagram Graph API, Facebook Graph API, and TikTok Content Posting API. Please refer to each platform's privacy policy for details on how they handle your data.
